## Slide 2: Introduction
- **What is Meal Craft AI?**
  - An intelligent web application that generates personalized recipes instantly.
  - Helps users create delicious meals using ingredients they already have.
- **The Problem:**
  - Wasting food because we don't know what to cook with leftover ingredients.
  - Difficulty in finding recipes that adhere to specific dietary restrictions.
  - Lack of time to decide on quick weeknight dinners.

## Slide 3: Key Features (Part 1)
- **Leftover Recipes:** Input random ingredients from your fridge, and the AI turns them into an actual meal.
- **Diet-Specific Customization:** Supports Keto, Vegan, Gluten-Free, High-Protein, and Vegetarian diets automatically.
- **Quick Weeknight Dinners:** Generates recipes that can be made in 30 minutes or less, tailored to your schedule.

## Slide 4: Key Features (Part 2)
- **Cuisine Explorer:** Pick any regional cuisine (e.g., North Indian, South Indian, Italian) and get authentic recipes.
- **Macro Nutritional Stats:** Detailed breakdown of calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fats for every generated recipe.
- **Save & Organize:** Save your favorite AI-generated recipes to your personal collection locally or to your user profile.

## Slide 5: How It Works
- **Step 1: Enter Ingredients / Cravings**
  - Type in what you have in your fridge or a specific dish you're craving.
- **Step 2: AI Generation**
  - Our smart AI engine analyzes your input, dietary preferences, and creates a step-by-step personalized recipe.
- **Step 3: Cook & Save**
  - Follow the precise instructions, view the macros, and save the recipe to your favorites for next time.

## Slide 6: Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React, Vite
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (for modern, responsive UI design)
- **Routing & State:** React Router DOM, Context API
- **AI Integration:** Advanced prompts and data processing to provide high-quality recipe output.
- **Other Tools:** React Icons, Axios for API calls.

## Slide 7: Why Meal Craft AI Stands Out
- **Smart Fallbacks:** Unlike standard tools, it effectively handles unusual ingredient combinations.
- **Local & Cloud Favorites Storage:** Access your saved recipes anytime.
- **Dark-First Responsive UI:** Sleek, modern design that works perfectly on desktop and mobile devices.
- **Chef Insights:** Access curated articles from professional chefs right within the platform.

## Slide 8: Future Scope
- **Pantry Inventory Tracking:** Automatically suggest recipes based on what's currently in your virtual pantry.
- **Social Sharing:** Share your AI creations with the Meal Craft AI community.
- **Grocery List Integration:** Automatically generate grocery lists for missing ingredients.

## Slide 9: Conclusion
- Meal Craft AI takes the stress out of meal planning, reduces food waste, and makes healthy, diet-specific cooking accessible to everyone.
- It’s like having a personal chef and nutritionist right in your pocket.
